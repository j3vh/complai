---
category: Article
article: Article 21  Corrective actions.md
---

Providers of [high-risk AI systems](high-risk AI systems.html) which consider or have reason to consider that a high-risk AI system which they have placed on the market or put into service is not in conformity with this Regulation shall immediately take the necessary corrective actions to bring that system into conformity, to withdraw it or to recall it, as appropriate. They shall inform the distributors of the high-risk AI system in question and, where applicable, the [authorised representative](authorised representative.html) and importers accordingly.
